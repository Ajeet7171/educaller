About Us

Welcome to aaryashedu.in

Aaryash Educational Services is an innovative Education Consultant
in Ghaziabad (Uttar Pradesh) providing solutions to students for
pursuing their Under Graduate and Post Graduate studies at
universities, colleges, and schools in India and overseas.
